MMA (Mixed Martial Arts)

MMA stands for Mixed Martial Arts and consist on a full-contact combat sport that allows striking and grappling, both while the opponent is standing or at the ground. This sport has a lot of combined techniques, following a mixed or combat sports and martial arts. Other combination of styles include Muay Thai , Brazilian Jiu Jitsu , Wrestling and Boxing. These are the characteristics off the sport that have given it a lot of popularity, making it one of the most fast-growing sports in the world. At Universal Combat London the classes will be conducted based on the mixture of all the above sports. Our coaches have got the experience and the patience to give the best out of them to the members of our club. During their classes they will cover a mixture of striking, clinch work, takedowns, throws etc. Come and visit us, you won’t be disappointed !

What you need to bring?

You only need to bring in shorts and t-shirt for the first class. The shorts need to be medium long and must have no zips or any metallic material. We will provide our member will all the other necessary equipment and essentials needed for them to secure their goods such as pad locks or clean towels during their classes
